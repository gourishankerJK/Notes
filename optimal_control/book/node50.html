3.3 Optimal control problem formulation and assumptions

Having completed our study of calculus of variations, we have now come full circle and are ready to tackle the optimal control problem posed at the very beginning of the book, in Section 1.1. The purpose of this section is to give a more detailed formulation of that problem, by discussing and comparing its several specific variants and spelling out necessary technical assumptions.
